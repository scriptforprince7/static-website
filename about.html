About Us

Welcome to ,
Saraswati Bio Trading

SARASWATI BIO TRADING is a reputable company specialising in the Trading, Supply, and Export of Herbal Nutraceutical, Herbal Food Supplements, Ayurvedic Medicines. Since our establishment in 2019, we have garnered recognition in the Ayurvedic medicine market due to our unwavering commitment to producing and delivering only the highest quality products.

Our success is attributed to our firm belief in the importance of superior product standards. We take great pride in our extensive knowledge of Herbal Nutraceutical, Herbal Food Supplements, Ayurvedic Medicines, including their research and preparation methods. This expertise is made possible by our esteemed team of M.D. Ayurveda Doctors and Ph.D. Ayurveda Doctors, who contribute their profound knowledge to our operations.

We are dedicated to upholding our reputation as a trusted provider of Herbal Nutraceutical, Herbal Food Supplements, Ayurvedic Medicines, and we remain steadfast in our mission to serve our customers with exceptional products that align with their health, well-being and business goals.

Dr. Ravi Raj M.D (Ayurveda) Rasa Shastra Evum Bhaishjaya Kalpana, an accomplished and highly regarded Associate Professor at SHRI KRISHNA AYUSH UNIVERSITY KURUKSHETRA (INDIA), is a prominent figure in the field of Herbal Nutraceutical, Herbal Food Supplements, Ayurvedic Drug Manufacturing and Development. I do have an Experience of Compounding, Formulating and Developing Herbal Nutraceutical, Herbal Food Supplements, Ayurvedic Medicines, for Human Use.
